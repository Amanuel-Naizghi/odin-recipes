Cheese Burger

Description

Ingredients

The following are the Ingredients of a Cheese Burger

  • Ground beef grilled
  • Two halves of a bun
  • Slices of raw onion
  • Lettuce, bacon
  • Cheese and other ingredients add flavor
Steps for making the dish
  1. Grind the meat
    • Its more recommended to ask the butcher to grind the meat for you
  2. Place the ground beef in a bowl
  3. Roughly chop the onion and garlic
    • Add ingredients like Worcestershire sauce, ketchup, mustard
  4. Add the egg yolk
  5. Mix it all together
  6. Create the burgers
    • Shape the mixture into 6 equal-sized balls using your hands.
    • Press the balls down to make flat burger shapes about ½ inch (1.27cm) thick.
    • Make a small indentation in the center of the burger with your thumb.
  7. Choose your cooking method
    • Broiler/grill: Preheat the broiler (upper level grill) to medium heat.
    • Frying pan or skillet: Add oil or butter to the pan and fry the burgers well.
    • Place on the barbecue grill. Cook as usual for hamburgers on the barbecue.
    • Bake: Place in the oven on 350ºF/180ºC for 15 to 30 minutes, depending on thickness.
  8. Cover each with the top half of its bun, and enjoy.
